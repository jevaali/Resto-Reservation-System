Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our restaurant reservation system.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, and restaurant preferences when you register or make a reservation.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process reservations, communicate with you about your bookings, and improve our service.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Please contact us for assistance.
